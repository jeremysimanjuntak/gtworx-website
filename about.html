Our Story

Built on
Passion.

GT WORX was founded by car enthusiasts who were frustrated with garages that didn't understand performance cars — or worse, didn't care. We built a workshop around the idea that every car deserves to be treated properly, and every customer deserves a straight answer.

Over 10 years later, we're one of the UK's most trusted independent performance workshops. We do everything from everyday MOTs to full Stage 2 builds — and we approach every job with the same standards.

"Your car, our expertise. No hidden costs. No waffle. Just the job done right."
