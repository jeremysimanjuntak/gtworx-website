Our Story

Built on
Passion.

GT WORX is an independent workshop specialising in performance modifications alongside everyday servicing and MOT work. The workshop was set up around custom exhaust fabrication and multi-brand diagnostic capability.

Day-to-day work covers ECU remapping, full and interim servicing, MOT testing, diagnostics and general mechanical repairs. Performance builds — from Stage 1 maps to full Stage 2 hardware — make up a regular share of the workshop's output.

How We Work

01
Itemised Quotes

Parts and labour are quoted separately before any work starts. Additional work found during a job is quoted and authorised before it's carried out.

02
In-House Capability

Diagnostics, exhaust fabrication, welding and most mechanical work is handled on-site. Vehicles are rarely sent to third parties.

03
Performance Specialism

Stage 1, Stage 2 and custom ECU mapping are a regular part of the workshop's output. Supporting hardware — exhausts, intercoolers, brakes — is specified and fitted in-house.

04
All Makes

Workshop services cover petrol and diesel vehicles across most makes — see the brands strip above for what we routinely work on.
