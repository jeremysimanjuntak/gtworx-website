Equipment

What We Run

Custom Exhaust Fab Bay

Dedicated TIG welding station for custom exhaust work. Mild steel and stainless. Resonator deletes, backbox deletes, custom midpipes, full bespoke systems.

Multi-Brand Diagnostics

Professional multi-brand diagnostic equipment covering European, Japanese, and Korean vehicles. Live data, fault reading, programming.

ECU Mapping Suite

Latest mapping hardware and software for OBD, bench, and boot-mode flashing. Supporting most petrol and diesel ECUs.

4-Post Ramps

Full-length ramps for safe, accessible underbody work. No shortcuts on access — the job gets done properly.

DVSA MOT Bay

Fully-equipped, DVSA-approved MOT testing lane for Class 4 and Class 7 vehicles. Annual compliance inspections passed.

Gas Analyser

Emissions testing and monitoring equipment as part of our MOT and diagnostic capability.

What We Build

  • Resonator deletes
    Removes the centre resonator to free up sound and reduce restriction.
  • Backbox deletes
    Tips welded direct to the midpipe for a deeper note and a cleaner rear look.
  • Custom midpipes & Y-pipes
    Bent and welded to suit existing or upgraded downpipes and rear sections.
  • Full bespoke systems
    From scratch — measured to your car, in your choice of material.
